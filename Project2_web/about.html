NATIONAL MICRO ENTERPRISE PITCHING COMPETITION 2024 organised by the Student Intake and Development Division(BAPP),JPKK and fully managed by Politeknik Sultan Abdul Halim Mu'Adzam Shah (POLIMAS),Kedah. The competition program commenced from October 18, 2024, to October 29, 2024.

A total of 435 entries were received from Polytechnic and Community Colleges across Malaysia, and the judging sessions took place from November 1 to November 3, 2024, involving 22 panels of judges consisting of local university lectures and MARA Higher Skills Colleges, led by Dr. Umar Hayat Abdul Kohar,Deputy Director of the Enterpreneuership Center, Universiti Teknologi Malaysia(UTM).
