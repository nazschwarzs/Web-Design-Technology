ACTIVITIES

JTMK (Information and Communication Technology Department) at POLIMAS is dedicated to providing our students with exciting and enriching experiences both in and out of the classroom. Our student program offers a variety of activities and events designed to complement your academic journey, develop new skills, and foster personal growth.

From workshops and seminars to hackathons and industry visits, our program covers a wide range of interests. Whether you're looking to expand your network, gain practical skills, or simply have fun, the JTMK Student Program has something for you.

INDUSTRIAL VISIT TO SOPHIC AUTOMATION SDN BHD

The Information and Communication Technology Department (JTMK) of POLIMAS organized an industrial visit to Sophic Automation Sdn Bhd in Bukit Mertajam, Penang on May 8th, 2024.

The purpose of this visit was to provide exposure to 5th-semester students, allowing them to gain theoretical and practical experience in the field of Automation and IoT before entering the workforce in the future.
