ABOUT

Intoduction of program

The Department of Information Technology & Communication (JTMK) is one of the major Academic Departments at Sultan Abdul Halim Mu'adzam Shah Polytechnic (POLIMAS), Jitra. The purpose, objectives, and learning outcomes of the Information Technology Diploma program are formulated based on the vision and mission of the Polytechnic and Community College Education Department (JPPKK). This program equips students with various skills to enable them to adapt to the challenging work environment upon completion of their studies. JTMK is equipped with the best and complete training and education facilities to support practical and theoretical learning outcomes for all courses, in line with Outcome-Based Education (OBE) requirements.

Synopsis Program

This programme provides education and training in computing field with a specific emphasis on Information Technology area. The courses offered is grouped into modules such as Platform Technologies, Computing Foundation, Programming Essentials, Industry-Led modules and Industry Domain Cluster, which are referred to specific task. The courses offered provide opportunities for students to get into a broad range of careers in a variety of ICT sectors. This programme also provides the Inventive Problem Solving to enhance their competencies in soft skills. As the programme emphasizes self-initiated learning and hands-on competencies, graduates of this programme will be ready to take the challenges in the world of computing technologies.
