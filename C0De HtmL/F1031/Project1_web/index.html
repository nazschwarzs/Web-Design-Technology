It is a program aimed at developing and nurturing students who are confident and possess the skills for 'pitching', which is the art of explaining the strengths and advantages and providing precise input regarding the products and services they offer in the market.

  • It assists students from Polytechnic and Community Colleges in showcasing their abilities and experiences in advancing.
  • It is conducted entirely online.

It prepares them to face the challenges of the global economic landscape and the technological changes associated directly with the Fourth Industrial Revolution, where students will be exposed to pitching methods both directly and in video format, considering that online promotional capabilities offer extensive space and opportunities for businesses.
